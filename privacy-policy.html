Privacy Policy

Last updated: 29 June 2026  ·  Effective from: 29 June 2026

This policy explains what personal data CoachApp collects, why, and your rights under UK GDPR. If you have questions, email jakendwest@gmail.com.

1. Who we are

CoachApp is operated by Jake West, a personal trainer based in the United Kingdom. For the purposes of UK data protection law, Jake West is the data controller.

Contact: jakendwest@gmail.com

2. What data we collect

We collect the following categories of personal data:

  • Account data — your name and email address, collected when you create an account or accept an invite.
  • Health and training data — body weight, body fat percentage, workout logs, exercise performance records, personal bests, and progress photographs. This is special category data under UK GDPR and is handled with additional care.
  • Goal and check-in data — goals you set, milestone notes, and weekly check-in responses.
  • Calendar events — session scheduling data you or your coach adds.
  • Usage data — we log errors and application events to diagnose technical issues. These logs contain only internal IDs and timestamps — never your name, email, or health values.

3. Why we collect it and our legal basis

  • To deliver the service — providing your training dashboard, workout tracking, and progress monitoring. Legal basis: contract performance.
  • Health and training data — processing special-category data requires explicit consent. You gave this when you ticked the consent checkbox at signup. Legal basis: explicit consent (Article 9(2)(a) UK GDPR).
  • To improve the service — anonymised usage patterns help us fix bugs and prioritise features. Legal basis: legitimate interests.

4. Where your data is stored

All data is stored in Supabase's EU (Ireland, eu-west-1) region. This is within the European Economic Area and meets UK GDPR adequacy requirements for data residency.

Progress photographs are stored in a private, access-controlled bucket. They are never publicly accessible.

5. Who we share your data with

We do not sell or share your data with third parties for marketing purposes. Data is shared only with:

  • Supabase Inc. — our database and storage provider, acting as a data processor under a data processing agreement.
  • Your coach — if you are a client account, your coach can view your training data, weight logs, goals, performance records, and progress photos as part of the personal training service.

6. How long we keep your data

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, all personal data — including health records, workout logs, and photographs — is permanently deleted within 30 days. Some anonymised, non-identifiable data may be retained for service improvement purposes.

7. Your rights under UK GDPR

You have the following rights:

  • Right of access — you can download a copy of all your personal data from Settings → Data & Privacy → Download my data.
  • Right to erasure — you can permanently delete your account and all associated data from Settings → Data & Privacy → Delete account.
  • Right to rectification — you can update your name and email from within the app.
  • Right to withdraw consent — you can withdraw consent for processing special-category health data at any time by deleting your account. This will not affect the lawfulness of processing before withdrawal.
  • Right to lodge a complaint — you have the right to lodge a complaint with the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) at ico.org.uk.

8. Cookies and local storage

CoachApp uses browser local storage to remember your preferred view (PT or client) between sessions. No advertising or tracking cookies are used.

9. Security

All data is transmitted over HTTPS. Database access is protected by row-level security policies — each user can only access their own data. Progress photographs require a time-limited signed URL to view and cannot be accessed directly.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect any changes. For significant changes, we will notify users by email.

11. Contact

For any privacy questions or to exercise your rights, contact: jakendwest@gmail.com
